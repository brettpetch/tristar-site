Services

Tri-Star Metal Stamping is a large Tier 2 Automotive supplier of stamped components running several presses accomodating up to 600 tons, and verying specifications of bolster sizes up to 144" by 60". Tool maintenance and repairs are expertly performed by professional toolmakers, in the fully equipped designated die maintenance area. In-house CMM capabilities allow for verification of our products and processes. Our Quality System will provide you with all QS9000/ ISO/ TS 16949 requirements including APQP and PPAP methodology.
